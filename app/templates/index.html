Attendance
Tracker

This is an application to calculate the exact attendance duration of a meeting attendee.


How to use:
1. Download attendance from Microsoft Teams
2. Upload the .csv, .xlsx, .xls file here
3. Indicate the meeting start and end times
4. Click on 'Go'
5. Preview and download the resulting file
